Toast Behavior

Stacking

  • Maximum 5 toasts visible at once
  • 8px gap between stacked toasts
  • New toasts appear at top of stack

Auto-dismiss

  • Configurable duration (3s, 5s, 10s)
  • Progress bar shows time remaining
  • Hover pauses the countdown timer

Animations

  • Slide-in animation: 200ms ease-in-out
  • Direction based on position
  • Smooth dismiss animation

Technical Specs

  • Min width: 300px, Max width: 420px
  • Z-index: 1100
  • Progress bar height: 3px
